## About

I am an interdisciplinary researcher working across accounting, business analytics, and artificial intelligence. My research centres on the credibility of corporate ESG disclosure — how sustainability claims can be extracted from multimodal reporting materials, linked to source evidence, and evaluated for consistency and greenwashing risk.

My current interests include ESG disclosure credibility, multimodal document understanding, causal inference, explainable machine learning, and AI-assisted sustainability assurance.

## Research Interests

- ESG disclosure credibility and greenwashing detection
- Multimodal sustainability reporting analysis
- Accounting research with AI and causal inference
- Explainable machine learning for corporate risk prediction
- Cross-market ESG disclosure consistency
- AI agents for research workflows and evidence tracing
